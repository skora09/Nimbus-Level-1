CloudWalk offers a robust benefits package designed for a remote workforce. This includes unlimited paid time off (PTO), a monthly stipend for home office setup and utilities, and comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance coverage.

We also offer a matching 401(k) retirement plan up to 4% of the employee's salary. Parental leave is set at 16 weeks fully paid for all primary caregivers. We encourage employees to take mental health days regularly to prevent burnout.
