All requests for expense reimbursement must be submitted through the internal "WalkFlow" platform within a maximum of 15 days of the expense date.

Reimbursable expenses include: authorized business travel, essential software licenses for work, and professional development costs (courses, conferences), limited to $2,000 per year.

To process the reimbursement, attaching the original tax receipt (invoice) is mandatory. Per diem allowances for meals during business travel are fixed at $50 per day, without the need for receipts.
